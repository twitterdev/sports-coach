Sports coach 🏈⚽🎾🕹👾

Make your sports related conversations knock it out of the park. This app lets you manage replies that are not sports related, based on Tweet annotations (Twitter’s artificial intelligence to detect topic and entities of a Tweet).

How to make Sports explorer work for you

  1. Ask your friends on Twitter to reply to your Tweet with a sport-related story or fact, or with a name of a sports team (including eSports).
  2. Paste a link to your Tweet (on Twitter, click on
    , then paste it in the field below).
  3. If the Tweet has replies, it will show you whether it contains sports-related replies or not, based on Twitter’s understanding of each reply.
  4. Have fun! Hide and unhide replies by following the app’s prompts.
